Chicken Platter


Recipie Description

Chiken platter is recipie containing marinated chiken with combination of spicy
normal rice with saugue of different varities

Ingredients
  • Marinated Chiken
  • Rice of two different varities
  • spices to enhance flavour
  • sausage of white, barbecque, mint and hot sauce
Step
  1. Marinate the chiken for about 4 hours
  2. add spices as in pepper, chilli paste
  3. garnish with giner galic paste
